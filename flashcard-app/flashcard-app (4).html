    How to Use FlashStudy

    Getting Started:

    • Choose a study mode (Review All, Learn New, etc.)
    • Click "Start Studying" to begin
    • Click on cards or press Space to flip them

    Rating Cards:

    • Easy: You remembered it quickly
    • Normal: Took some thought but got it
    • Hard: Struggled to remember
    • Forget: Couldn't recall at all

    Tips:

    • Use keyboard shortcuts for faster studying
    • Mark important cards with the bookmark (⭐)
    • Enable audio to hear questions/answers read aloud
    • Review difficult cards more frequently
